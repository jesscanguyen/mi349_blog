snacking on chocolate-covered strawberries

published: 10/10/24

jessica nguyen

for a long time now, i've been absolutely obsessed with chocolate-covered strawberries! they are one of my most favorite ever snacks/sweet treats. they contrast each other so well: the refreshing sweet taste of a fruit, complemented by the thick sweet chocolate. although i am not the best at describing it, i still stand by the fact that it's a taste to die for.

if i'm not making them from scratch myself, i usually get mine frozen from a brand called tru fru! they have a lot of fruit-centered snacks, but of course, i love their milk-chocolate covered strawberries the most. i discovered them when i was at target with my boyfriend: we were looking in the frozen aisle for some treats until a person came rushing over to the same section as us. as she faced the section, she excitedly shouted, "here they are!" and reached in for one of the packs of frozen treats from tru fru. soon enough, her friend followed behind and funnily rebutted, "this is what we came here for?" in which the original person said, "yes! these are so good you don't understand." after seeing that exchange, my curiousity was piqued, and i grabbed a pack for myself. there were two to choose from: chocolate-covered raspberries or strawberries, but i chose the latter since it is one of my favorite fruits.

and the rest is history! i honestly haven't gotten sick of them at all, which surprises me. sure, i'll have a week or so break from eating them, but i always crawl back at the end.
